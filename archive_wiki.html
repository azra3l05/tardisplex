Sign Up for Plex

  • Visit the Plex website at plex.tv and create an account.
  • Contact the admin to receive access to TheArchive's Plex server. Provide your Plex account email for whitelisting.
  • Once approved, log in to Plex and access TheArchive's library under "Shared Libraries."

Setting Up Your Devices for Plex

Supported Devices

  • Smart TVs with Plex app support.
  • Streaming devices like Roku, Apple TV, Amazon Fire Stick, and Chromecast.
  • Mobile devices running iOS or Android.
  • Desktop and laptop computers via the Plex Web app.

Steps to Set Up Plex

  • Download and install the Plex app on your preferred device.
  • Log in to your Plex account.
  • Navigate to "Shared Libraries" and select TheArchive server.
  • Browse and enjoy your media collection!

Tips for Optimal Performance

  • Ensure a stable internet connection for seamless streaming.
  • Enable hardware acceleration in the Plex app settings if supported by your device.
  • Update the Plex app regularly to access the latest features and improvements.

Request Process through Overseerr

  • Log in to Overseerr at overseerr.tardisonline.in.
  • Search for the desired movie or TV show in the search bar.
  • If available, click on the "Request" button to add it to the request queue.
  • Wait for admin approval. Once approved, the content will be added to TheArchive library.
  • Check the status of your requests on the "Requests" page within Overseerr.

Key Features

  • Dual Instances of Plex and Emby for redundancy and personalized libraries.
  • Comprehensive Media Insights through Tautulli.
  • Streamlined Media Requests using Overseerr.
  • Centralized Dashboard for seamless navigation between services.
